Playlist

Attributes:

number_of_tracks (integer)
playlist_name (string)
current_song (string)
genre (array)
is_paused (boolean)


count_tracks (returns number_of_tracks in playlist)
play (change is_paused to "false")
add_twang (adds country_music to beginning of genre array)
add_new_album (adds 20 to number_of_tracks)
